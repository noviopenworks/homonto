# /grill

Stress-test the plan by interviewing me until we reach a shared understanding. If
the `grilling` skill is installed, follow it; otherwise apply the same contract
below.

Walk the design as a tree — settle upstream decisions before the choices that
depend on them. Ask one question at a time and wait for my answer before the
next. For each question, recommend an answer with its one main reason and the
key trade-off.

Look up facts in the codebase yourself; put only real decisions to me. Spend the
questions where reversal is expensive, and say when you're skipping a cheap one.
Keep a running list of settled decisions so a reset can't lose them.

Do not enact the plan. When the open questions are low-stakes or I call it,
replay every decision as a numbered summary and wait for my explicit
confirmation.

`$ARGUMENTS`, if present, is the plan or design to grill.
